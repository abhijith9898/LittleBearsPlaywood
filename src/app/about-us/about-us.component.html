About Us

`Ever since the COVID-19 pandemic we have noticed that many kids are not playing and that some are usually in front of screens. So, we wanted to change this. We came up with this toy renting-business to help children imagine, create, have fun and help them think out of the box.

Our Inspiration

  • In the past few years, we have noticed kids becoming addicted to screens making them socially unhealthy and forgetful of play. The value of traditional play is not being imparted properly to the next generation. We believe that play can touch a child’s heart and deeply enhance his growth. Our toys are thus hardly technology based allowing development of other capabilities.
  • Alongside that, we have been concerned about the cluttering of materials as a result of excessive purchase and eventual idleness of toys. From a baby’s first crawl, Mother Earth bears the weight of our actions.
  • By being exemplary in the field of environmental sustainability to our little bears, we are sowing the seeds that one day will become trees of awareness and power. Bearing in mind that the future of our globe is clasped within our children’s hands, we have to work together to nurture the right ideas and thoughts in them.
